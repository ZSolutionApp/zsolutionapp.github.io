Privacy Policy

Introduction

ZSolution ("we", "our", or "us") offers the "Dynamic Button Links" application ("the Service") to merchants utilizing Shopify to manage their stores. This Privacy Policy outlines how personal information is collected, used, and shared when you install or use the App in conjunction with your Shopify-supported store.

Personal Information the App Collects

Information from Merchants

Upon installation of the App, we are automatically able to access certain information from your Shopify account, such as:

  • Store name
  • Store ID
  • Store owner's email address
  • Installation and uninstallation dates and times

This data is utilized to facilitate and improve the Service, confirm installations and uninstalls, and communicate with you for support if necessary.

Information from Merchant’s Customers and Visitors of the Merchant’s Store

Our App does not collect, process, or retain any personal data from customers or visitors of your Shopify store.

Information from Cookies and Similar Tracking Technologies

We do not employ cookies or other tracking technologies to gather personal information.

How We Use Your Personal Information

The personal information collected from you is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Communicate with you
  • Optimize or improve the App
  • Provide you with information or advertising related to our products or services (if applicable)

Sharing Your Personal Information

Your personal data is not shared with third parties for advertising purposes. However, we may share your personal information to comply with legal requirements, respond to subpoenas, search warrants, or other lawful requests for information we receive, or to protect our rights.
